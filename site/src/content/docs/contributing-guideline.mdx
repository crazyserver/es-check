## Guidelines for contibutors

1. **Fork** the repository and **clone** it to your local machine.

2. **Create a branch** for your contribution. Use a descriptive name for your branch.

3. **Commit** your changes with clear and concise messages.

4. **Push** your changes to your fork.

5. **Submit a pull request** (PR) to the `main` branch of the original repository.

6. Ensure your PR includes:
   - A descriptive title summarizing the changes.
   - Detailed information in the description about the problem and solution.

7. **Be responsive** to feedback and comments on your PR. Make any requested changes promptly.

8. Before submitting your PR, ensure:
   - Your code follows the project's style guidelines.
   - You have included relevant tests (if applicable).
   - You have updated documentation (if necessary).

9. By contributing, you agree that your work will be licensed under the project's license.

Thank you for your contribution!

If you have any questions or need further assistance, feel free to reach out to us. We're here to help!
